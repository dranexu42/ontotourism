Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

This ontology models key concepts related to cultural heritage, including cities, museums, monuments, and transport systems. The vocabulary includes core classes and properties for describing cultural entities, their locations, and relationships. It is designed for use by students, institutions, and developers working on linked open data applications. The ontology also reuses selected terms from DBpedia to enhance interoperability.
